About the project

Hooklist is being developed as a software product focused on creative workflow support. The goal is to help users save public references, add context, and use them as a basis for future ideas and content planning.

What Hooklist is for

  • Saving links and creative references in one place.
  • Organizing ideas by concept, topic, or campaign.
  • Adding notes and context to references for future reuse.
  • Supporting structured creative workflows instead of scattered bookmarks.

Current status

  • Early-stage software product in development.
  • Focused on reference management and idea organization.
  • Intended for creators, marketers, and users working with inspiration and content research.

Core product areas

The product centers around collecting references, structuring them, and making them easier to reuse in practical creative work.

Reference capture

Users can save public content links as references for future review and organization.

Idea organization

References can be grouped with notes, themes, and internal context for easier retrieval.

Creative workflow

Saved materials are intended to support ideation, planning, and content preparation.
